What we're fighting for

As an EMS advocate and reform leader, I've spent years fighting for EMTs, paramedics, patients, and taxpayers. Now, I'm taking that fight to Washington.

Strengthen EMS as Essential Healthcare

  • Recognize EMS as a vital part of our healthcare system.
  • Increase support for EMT and paramedic recruitment and retention.
  • Invest in emergency response infrastructure that saves lives and taxpayer dollars.

Protect EMTs and Paramedics

  • Fight for competitive municipal and state pay for EMS professionals.
  • Support civil service protections for EMTs and paramedics.
  • Ensure first responders have the resources and protections they deserve.

End Ambulance Monopolies

  • Protect patient choice in ambulance services, especially for bed-confined patients.
  • Promote competition, transparency, and accountability in emergency medical transportation.
  • Fight to remove monopoly protections that limit patient options and innovation.

Bring Louisiana's Voice to Washington

  • Advocate for policies that improve emergency care and public safety.
  • Deliver practical solutions instead of partisan politics.
  • Ensure Louisiana's first responders and working families have a voice in Congress.

Why I'm running

A lifetime of service.
A future of reform.

I've seen firsthand what works — and what doesn't — in emergency medicine. I believe government should serve the people, reward innovation, and be accountable to taxpayers. Now I'm taking that fight to Washington to deliver real results for Louisiana and our first responders.
